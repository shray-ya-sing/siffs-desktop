# Alibaba Quarterly Income Statement - Other Income/Expense Summary

## Key Items

### Other Income (Expense), Net
- Primarily consists of interest income, foreign exchange gains/losses, and other non-operating items
- Shows some volatility quarter-to-quarter, reflecting changes in cash balances and currency movements

## Observations
- The "Other income (expense), net" line item aggregates various non-operating financial items
- These amounts are typically smaller than core operating items but can impact net income
- The data shows this is a recurring line item in the income statement

## Dependencies
- This line item feeds into the calculation of income before income taxes
- The values here would be combined with operating income to arrive at pre-tax income
- Changes in this item should be analyzed in the context of the company's cash position and foreign currency exposure
